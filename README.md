**BTC Cycles Simulator** is a high-fidelity educational tool designed to visualize the impact of time, inflation, and strategic decisions on Bitcoin accumulation over 4-year cycles.

Inspired by modern digital banking and crypto interfaces, this simulator allows users to project their financial freedom using mathematical models of compound interest and currency devaluation.

### 1. üçï Freedom Tracker (Freedom Levels)
Visualize how many years of your current lifestyle are covered by your Bitcoin stack.
*   **Survival (6m):** Covers your immediate basic needs.

*   **Independence (10y):** Freedom to choose what to work on.

*   **Automatic DCA:** Configure recurring monthly savings that accumulate automatically.
